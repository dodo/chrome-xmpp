Download chrome-xmpp extension

chrome-xmpp is an extension which exposes an easy direct access from any website to xmpp …
… and gives you back full privacy and full control over your accounts.

To gain full potential out of chrome-xmpp, please install all three extensions.
(Because of browser extension security reasons this has to be 3 extensions, sadly)

backend

The backend manages all the xmpp connections for you.
It comes without any interface and is pretty much useless when installed alone.

chrome-xmpp-backend.crx chrome-xmpp-backend.zip

frontend

The frontend provides options to manage your accounts.
You also get a browser action button to quick access all your accounts and their options.

chrome-xmpp.crx chrome-xmpp.zip

page status addon

This addon gives you a small icon displaying the state of the current tab's accounts whether their online or not.

chrome-xmpp-page-status.crx chrome-xmpp-page-status.zip
